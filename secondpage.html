Herbs and how to use them

List of Herbs: Spearmint, Sage, & Rosemary.

Spearmint

Spearmint(Mentha spicata l.) is known for it's ability to help alleviate indegetion, gas, and nausea. As well as helping to relieve certain sypmtoms of IBS and PCOS.

Sage

Sage(Salvia Officinalis) has properties that help improve brain function via cognitive and memory boosts, & lower blood sugar to name a few.

Rosemary

Rosemary(Salvia Rosmarinus) has properties that can aide in hair growth and repair, provide an immune system boost, & can improve blood circulation. Avoid high supplement doses as this can lead to nausea and vomiting.
